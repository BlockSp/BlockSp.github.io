pGMRES with block SOR preconditioner/LU block inversion.

SOR damping parameter of 1.6, 3 iterations per direction (6 total), symmetric SOR. Note: The SOR damping parameter w may greatly impact solver speed. w = (0, 2), larger parameters are often faster but smaller are more robust. Note: Not restarted by default.
